Mastering options

0 dB and Normal are the default controls, but the initial master still makes automatic analysis-based improvements. These controls let you steer the result.

Lo EQ
0.0 dB
Low shelf adjustment for weight and low-end balance.
Mid EQ
0.0 dB
Broad midrange contour for clarity and body.
High EQ
0.0 dB

Loudness
Normal

Normal
